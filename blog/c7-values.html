Values

June 14, 2015

Accountability - so much of the time this is interpreted as “blame” so we defensively say or think “it’s wasn’t my fault”. I am human and "to err” is part of our lives. As is such, I know I will make mistakes but when I own them, learn from them, and grow from them, I feel like I walk taller.
